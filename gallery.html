My Gallery


This gallery section is a glimpse into my life as an Arts and Design student — moments that capture both the beauty and the hard work behind every creative piece.

You’ll see photos from our theatre rehearsals and performances — each one representing a story, a lesson, and a memory worth keeping. From painting backdrops and designing costumes to performing live on stage, every experience has shaped my journey as an artist.

Each project is more than just a grade — it’s a reflection of dedication, teamwork, and passion. I’m proud to share these works because they remind me of how far I’ve come, and how much more there is to learn and create.
